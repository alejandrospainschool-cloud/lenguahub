# 🎓 LenguaHub: Product Overview & Pitch

## The Problem We Solve

Spanish tutors and teachers spend hours managing scattered tools:
- 📝 Spreadsheets for student tracking
- 📱 Multiple apps for assignments
- 📊 Manual analytics for progress
- 💬 Endless emails for coordination

**Result**: Less time teaching, more time managing.

**LenguaHub Solution**: One unified platform where teachers see every student's Spanish learning journey in real-time.

---

## 🎯 The Product

### For Individual Learners
- 🗣️ AI-powered Spanish learning (vocabulary, grammar, conversation)
- 📚 Build your personal word bank with custom translations
- 📅 Calendar for scheduling practice
- 🎮 Gamification (streaks, levels, achievements)
- **Price**: £9.99/mo or free (freemium)

### For Tutors (What You Get All This + Team Features)
- 👥 Manage up to 10 (lite) or unlimited (pro) students
- 📊 Real-time progress dashboards
- 📝 Lesson tracking and assignment management
- 🎯 Student-level analytics
- 💬 Built-in feedback system
- **Prices**: 
  - Lite: £50/mo (up to 10 students)
  - Pro: £100/mo (unlimited students)

### For Schools
- 🏫 Unlimited teachers and students
- 🎓 Class-based organization
- 📊 Full institutional analytics
- 🔗 Integration-ready APIs
- ✅ GDPR compliance built-in
- 📥 Bulk student import
- **Price**: £5 per student/month (minimal cost per learner)

---

## 💰 Revenue Model

**Three Independent Revenue Streams:**

### 1. Individual Users (Freemium)
- Free: Limited features, ads
- Paid: £9.99/mo full access
- Expected conversion: 5-10% of active users

### 2. Tutors (Subscription)
- Lite: £50/mo × estimated 1,000 tutors (year 1) = £600k/year
- Pro: £100/mo × estimated 200 tutors (year 1) = £240k/year

### 3. Schools (Per-Student)
- £5/student/month × 500 students (1-2 schools) (year 1) = £30k/year
- **High expansion potential**: Spanish departments in every English school

## 📞 Questions to Prepare Answers For

**"What makes this better than Duolingo for Schools?"**
- Duolingo is a learning tool. We're a **management platform** for teachers.
- Duolingo has no way for teachers to assign homework or see custom progress.
- We include Duolingo-like learning PLUS tutor tools.

**"How is this different from ClassDojo / Clever / Google Classroom?"**
- Those are **generic** classroom tools. We're **Spanish-specific**.
- We have built-in AI translation, verb conjugation practice, vocabulary tracking.
- Teachers can focus on teaching, not building their own materials.

**"What's your competitive advantage?"**
- **Specificity**: Built for Spanish teachers by a Spanish teacher.
- **Price**: £5/student vs $15-50/student for alternatives.
- **Ease**: Genuinely works great. No painful onboarding.

**"How do you monetize?"**
- Freemium for individual learners
