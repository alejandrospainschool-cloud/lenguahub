# 🚀 Lesson Logging - Quick Start Guide

## For Students

### How to Log a Lesson

1. **Go to Dashboard**
   - You'll see a new "This Month's Lessons" card

2. **Click "Log Lesson"**
   - A modal will pop up

3. **Fill in the form:**
   - **Lesson Type**: Choose from 6 options (Conversation, Grammar, Reading, Writing, Listening, Mixed)
   - **Lesson Title**: Give your lesson a name (e.g., "Preterite tense practice")
   - **Notes** (optional): Add what you learned or challenges faced
   - Duration is automatically set to 1 hour

4. **Click "✓ Log Lesson"**
   - Your lesson is saved instantly
   - Progress card updates automatically
   - You'll see a confirmation message

### Track Your Progress

- **Progress Card shows**: X/8 lessons this month
- **Color coding**:
  - 🟦 Blue: Regular progress (0-5 lessons)
  - 🟧 Amber: Almost at payment threshold (6-7 lessons)
  - 🟩 Green: Payment due (8 lessons reached)

### View Your Lessons

- Go to **Calendar** tab
- See your full lesson history
- Read teacher feedback on each lesson
- Add your own notes to reflect on lessons

## For Teachers

### Monitor Student Progress

1. **Go to Teacher Dashboard**
   - Click on **Students** tab

2. **Click on a Student**
   - You'll see a detailed view

3. **Scroll to "📚 Lessons This Month"**
   - See their progress: X/8 lessons
   - Progress bar shows payment status
   - Color-coded indicators:
     - 🟩 Green: Payment due
     - 🟧 Amber: Almost at threshold
     - 🟦 Blue: Regular progress

### View Student Lessons

- In the student details, scroll to **"Lessons"** section
- See recent lessons with dates
- See student notes about what they learned
- View total lessons logged

### Track Payment Timeline

- At a glance see which students will need payment soon
- Plan your schedule around the 8-lesson threshold
- Manage multiple students' payment cycles

## Troubleshooting

### My progr

ess didn't update
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure lesson has a valid date

### I can't log a lesson
- Title is required (can't be empty)
- May have exceeded daily log limit (contact admin if issue)
- Check browser console for errors

### Teacher can't see student lessons
- Make sure you're in proper teacher view (click "View Lessons" button)
- Student must have lessons logged with status "logged"
- Check that you're viewing the correct student

### Lessons disappeared
- They may not be from the current month (system filters by calendar month)
- Check the dates on your lessons
- Check if student deleted them

## FAQs

**Q: Do I have to log every lesson?**
A: No, but it helps track progress. Teachers get a better picture of your learning.

**Q: Can I edit a lesson after logging?**
A: Go to Calendar → find the lesson →click edit (if you see the button)

**Q: Can I log lessons from past days?**
A: Yes, when you log a lesson the system lets you choose the date.

**Q: What happens after 8 lessons?**
A: Teacher will see payment is due. For students on a plan, this triggers the billing cycle.

**Q: Do lessons reset monthly?**
A: Yes, automatically. New month = new counter starting at 0/8.

**Q: Can I add notes to lessons?**
A: Yes! Both students and teachers can add notes that the other can see.

## Quick Tips

### For Students
💡 Log lessons **soon after finishing** - you'll remember better what you learned
💡 Use **lesson types** to track different skills
💡 Add **personal notes** - your teacher might give feedback!

### For Teachers
💡 Check frequently to see **who's keeping up** with lessons
💡 **Add feedback notes** to encourage students
💡 Plan based on the **8-lesson cycle** for payment planning
